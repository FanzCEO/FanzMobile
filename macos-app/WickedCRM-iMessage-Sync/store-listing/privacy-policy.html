WickedCRM iMessage Sync Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

WickedCRM iMessage Sync ("we", "our", or "the app") is a macOS menu bar application that syncs your iMessages to your WickedCRM account. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

iMessage Data

With your permission (Full Disk Access), we read your iMessage database to:

  • Sync message content to your WickedCRM account
  • Extract contact information from conversations
  • Track sync progress locally

Connection Information

We store locally on your device:

  • Your WickedCRM server URL
  • API key (if configured)
  • Sync preferences
  • Last sync timestamp

How We Use Your Data

  • Message Sync: To transfer messages to your WickedCRM server
  • AI Processing: Your WickedCRM server may process messages for insights
  • Contact Matching: To associate messages with contacts

Data Storage

  • Settings are stored locally in macOS preferences
  • Messages are transmitted to your configured WickedCRM server
  • We do not store messages on any third-party servers
  • Your WickedCRM server handles data according to its own policies

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. Data is only sent to:

  • Your self-configured WickedCRM server

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete Server Data: Use the "Delete My Data" button in the app to remove all synced data from your WickedCRM server
  • Reset Sync: Clear sync history and start fresh
  • Disable Sync: Turn off automatic sync at any time
  • Uninstall: Remove the app to stop all data access

Permissions Required

  • Full Disk Access: Required to read the iMessage database at ~/Library/Messages/chat.db
  • Network Access: To communicate with your WickedCRM server

Security

  • The app runs with macOS App Sandbox protections
  • API keys are stored securely in macOS preferences
  • All network communication uses HTTPS

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
