## App Store Description

**Seamlessly sync your iMessages to WickedCRM for AI-powered message management.**

WickedCRM iMessage Sync is a lightweight menu bar app that automatically syncs your iMessages to your WickedCRM account. Never lose track of important conversations again.

**Key Features:**

• **Automatic Sync** - Messages sync in real-time to your WickedCRM dashboard
• **Menu Bar App** - Lives quietly in your menu bar, always ready
• **AI Processing** - Messages are analyzed for meetings, tasks, and contacts
• **Privacy First** - Your data stays encrypted and under your control
• **Easy Setup** - Connect to your WickedCRM account in seconds

**Perfect For:**
- Sales professionals managing client communications
- Small business owners tracking customer conversations
- Anyone who needs to keep messages organized

**How It Works:**
1. Install the app and grant Full Disk Access
2. Enter your WickedCRM server URL
3. Messages sync automatically in the background
4. View and manage all messages in your WickedCRM dashboard

**Privacy & Security:**
- End-to-end encryption for synced messages
- Delete your data anytime from Settings
- No data sold to third parties
- Full GDPR/CCPA compliance

**Requirements:**
- macOS 13.0 or later
- Full Disk Access permission (to read iMessage database)
- WickedCRM account (free tier available)
