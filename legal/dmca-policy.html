DMCA Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

FANZ Group Holdings LLC ("WickedCRM") respects intellectual property rights and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

1. Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed, send a notice to our DMCA Agent containing:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work
  3. Identification of the infringing material and its location
  4. Your contact information (address, phone, email)
  5. A statement that you believe in good faith the use is not authorized
  6. A statement under penalty of perjury that the information is accurate and you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

3. Counter-Notification

If your content was removed and you believe it was not infringing, you may submit a counter-notification containing:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature
  2. Identification of the removed material and its prior location
  3. A statement under penalty of perjury that removal was a mistake
  4. Your name, address, phone number
  5. Consent to jurisdiction of federal court in Wyoming
  6. Statement that you will accept service of process from the complainant

4. Repeat Infringers

We will terminate accounts of users who are repeat copyright infringers.

5. Good Faith

Please consider fair use and other limitations before filing a DMCA notice. Misrepresentation can result in liability for damages.
