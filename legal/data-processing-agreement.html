Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Effective Date: December 15, 2024

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between FANZ Group Holdings LLC ("Processor" / "WickedCRM") and you ("Controller" / "Customer").

1. Definitions

  • "Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
  • "Processing" means any operation performed on Personal Data
  • "Data Subject" means the individual to whom Personal Data relates
  • "Sub-processor" means any third party engaged by Processor to process Personal Data

2. Scope and Purpose

This DPA applies when WickedCRM processes Personal Data on behalf of the Customer to provide the Service. Processing is limited to:

  • Storing and displaying messages and contacts
  • AI analysis of message content
  • Spam detection and filtering
  • Analytics and service improvement

3. Customer Responsibilities

As Controller, Customer shall:

  • Ensure legal basis for processing
  • Provide required notices to Data Subjects
  • Respond to Data Subject requests
  • Ensure accuracy of Personal Data

4. Processor Obligations

WickedCRM shall:

  • Process Personal Data only on documented instructions
  • Ensure personnel are bound by confidentiality
  • Implement appropriate security measures
  • Assist Customer with Data Subject requests
  • Delete or return data upon termination
  • Allow and contribute to audits

6. International Transfers

For transfers outside the EEA/UK, we implement:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Transfer Impact Assessments where required
  • Supplementary measures as needed

7. Security Measures

We implement:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Incident response procedures
  • Employee security training

8. Data Breach Notification

We will notify you of a Personal Data breach without undue delay (within 72 hours where feasible) and provide:

  • Nature of the breach
  • Categories and number of Data Subjects affected
  • Likely consequences
  • Measures taken or proposed

9. Data Subject Rights

We assist with requests for:

  • Access to Personal Data
  • Rectification of inaccurate data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing

10. Audits

Upon reasonable notice, we will:

  • Provide information to demonstrate compliance
  • Allow audits by Customer or authorized auditor
  • Contribute to audits as reasonably requested

11. Term and Termination

This DPA continues while we process Personal Data. Upon termination, we will delete or return all Personal Data within 30 days unless retention is required by law.

For DPA inquiries: dpa@wickedcrm.com
