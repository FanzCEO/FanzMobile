Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of WickedCRM services. Violations may result in account suspension or termination.

1. Prohibited Content

You may not use our Service to store, transmit, or process:

  • Illegal Content: Content that violates any law or regulation
  • Harmful Content: Malware, viruses, or destructive code
  • Infringing Content: Content that violates intellectual property rights
  • Abusive Content: Harassment, threats, or hate speech
  • Fraudulent Content: Scams, phishing, or deceptive practices
  • CSAM: Child sexual abuse material (immediately reported to authorities)
  • Violent Content: Graphic violence or terrorism promotion

2. Prohibited Activities

  • Spamming: Sending unsolicited bulk messages
  • Hacking: Unauthorized access attempts
  • DDoS: Denial of service attacks
  • Scraping: Automated data extraction
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be someone else
  • Account Sharing: Sharing credentials with others
  • Circumvention: Bypassing security measures or usage limits
  • Reselling: Unauthorized resale of our services

3. Messaging Guidelines

When using our messaging features:

  • Only contact people who have consented to receive messages
  • Comply with TCPA, GDPR, and other messaging laws
  • Honor opt-out requests immediately
  • Include identification in commercial messages
  • Do not send messages during prohibited hours

4. API Usage

If using our API:

  • Respect rate limits and quotas
  • Do not share API keys
  • Implement proper error handling
  • Do not overload our systems

5. Resource Limits

We reserve the right to limit usage that:

  • Impacts service performance for others
  • Exceeds reasonable use patterns
  • Appears to be automated abuse

6. Reporting Violations

Report AUP violations to: abuse@wickedcrm.com

Include:

  • Description of the violation
  • Evidence (screenshots, logs)
  • Your contact information

7. Enforcement

Violations may result in:

  • Warning notification
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent termination
  • Legal action
  • Reporting to law enforcement

8. Appeals

To appeal an enforcement action, contact legal@wickedcrm.com within 30 days.
