WickedCRM Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

WickedCRM ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered CRM application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address for account creation
  • Name and profile information you provide
  • Authentication credentials (securely hashed)

Messages and Communications

  • Messages synced from connected channels (SMS, iMessage, email)
  • Contact information from your address book
  • Conversation metadata (timestamps, read status)

Usage Data

  • App usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

How We Use Your Data

  • CRM Features: To display and organize your messages and contacts
  • AI Processing: To analyze messages for meetings, tasks, and insights
  • Personalization: To customize your experience
  • Spam Detection: To filter unwanted messages
  • Improvements: To enhance our AI models and features

AI Processing

We use artificial intelligence to:

  • Extract meeting times and locations from messages
  • Identify action items and tasks
  • Classify message intent and importance
  • Generate smart reply suggestions

AI processing may use third-party services (e.g., OpenAI). Your data is processed according to their privacy policies as well.

Data Storage

  • Data is stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • Backups are encrypted and stored separately
  • Data is retained until you delete it or your account

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • AI Providers: For message processing (anonymized where possible)
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law

Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Export: Download a copy of your data (Settings → Export Data)
  • Delete: Permanently delete your data (Settings → Delete Account)
  • Correction: Update your information at any time
  • Opt-out: Disable specific features or data processing

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  1. Open WickedCRM
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy
  3. Select "Delete My Account" or "Delete All Data"
  4. Confirm deletion

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You can also request deletion by contacting privacy@wickedcrm.com.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • OpenAI (for AI processing)
  • Apple Push Notifications (for alerts)
  • Analytics services (anonymized usage data)

Security

  • All data transmitted using TLS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and monitoring

Children's Privacy

WickedCRM is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

International Users

Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
