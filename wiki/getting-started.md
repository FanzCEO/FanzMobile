# Getting Started with WickedCRM

Welcome! This guide will help you set up WickedCRM and start managing your communications.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

### On Mobile (iOS/Android)
1. Download WickedCRM from the App Store or Google Play
2. Tap **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email address or phone number
4. Create a secure password
5. Verify your account via email or SMS

### On Web
1. Visit [app.wickedcrm.com](https://app.wickedcrm.com)
2. Click **Get Started**
3. Follow the registration prompts

## Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

1. Add your name and profile photo
2. Set your timezone for accurate timestamps
3. Configure notification preferences

## Step 3: Connect Your Messages

### SMS (Android)
1. Install **WickedCRM SMS** from Google Play
2. Grant SMS permissions when prompted
3. Set as your default SMS app
4. Messages will automatically sync

### iMessage (Mac)
1. Install **WickedCRM iMessage Sync** from the Mac App Store
2. Grant Full Disk Access permission
3. Enter your WickedCRM server URL
4. Messages sync automatically
