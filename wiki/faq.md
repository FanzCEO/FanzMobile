## Account & Security

### How do I reset my password?
1. Go to the login screen
2. Tap "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email or phone number
4. Follow the reset link sent to you

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We use:
- TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Regular security audits
- SOC 2 Type II compliance (in progress)

### Can I use two-factor authentication?
Yes, enable 2FA in Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

## Messages & Sync

### Why aren't my messages syncing?
Check these common issues:
1. **Permissions**: Ensure SMS/messaging permissions are granted
2. **Internet**: Verify you have an internet connection
3. **Default App**: Make sure WickedCRM SMS is your default messaging app (Android)
4. **Full Disk Access**: Required for iMessage sync on Mac

### Can I sync messages from multiple devices?
Yes, install the appropriate app on each device:
- Android phone: WickedCRM SMS
- Mac: WickedCRM iMessage Sync
- Web: Access synced messages at app.wickedcrm.com

### Are my messages end-to-end encrypted?
Messages are encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). End-to-end encryption for messages within WickedCRM is coming soon.

### How far back does message sync go?
Initial sync includes all available message history. Subsequent syncs capture new messages automatically.

## AI Features

### What does the AI analyze?
Our AI extracts:
- Meeting times and locations
- Tasks and action items
- Contact names and numbers
- Message intent and importance

### How accurate is the AI?
AI accuracy varies by message complexity. We continuously improve our models. Always verify important information.

### Can I disable AI processing?
Yes, go to Settings → Privacy → AI Processing and toggle it off.

### Does AI read all my messages?
Only if you enable AI features. You control which messages are processed.

## Troubleshooting

### The app is crashing
1. Force close and reopen the app
2. Check for updates in the app store
3. Restart your device
4. If persists, contact support

### AI analysis isn't showing
- Ensure AI features are enabled in Settings
- Check your subscription includes AI features
- Try manually refreshing the message

### I'm not receiving notifications
1. Check notification permissions in device settings
2. Verify notifications are enabled in app Settings
3. Check Do Not Disturb isn't blocking notifications

### Sync is very slow
- Check your internet connection speed
- Large initial syncs may take time
- Contact support if sync takes over 24 hours
