## What is WickedCRM?

WickedCRM is an AI-powered Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform that helps you:

- **Unify Communications**: Bring all your messages (SMS, iMessage, email) into one inbox
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Automatically extract meetings, tasks, and important information
- **Contact Management**: Keep track of all your relationships and conversations
- **Spam Protection**: AI-powered filtering to keep unwanted messages out
- **Workflow Automation**: Automate repetitive tasks and follow-ups
