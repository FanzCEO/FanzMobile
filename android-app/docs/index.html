WickedCRM SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

WickedCRM SMS ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our SMS messaging application.

Information We Collect

SMS and MMS Messages

As a default SMS application, we access your SMS and MMS messages to:

  • Display your conversations in the app
  • Send and receive text messages on your behalf
  • Optionally sync messages to your WickedCRM account (if enabled)

Contact Information

We access your contacts to display names in conversations. Contact data is not uploaded to any server.

Phone Number Verification

When you receive a message, we may check the sender's phone number against spam databases to protect you from unwanted messages. Only the phone number is transmitted for this check, not message content.

How We Use Your Data

  • Message Display: To show your SMS/MMS conversations
  • Spam Protection: To identify and block spam messages
  • AI Processing: To extract meeting times, contacts, and tasks (processed locally or via your WickedCRM account)
  • Cloud Sync: If enabled, to backup messages to your WickedCRM account

Data Storage

  • Messages are stored locally on your device in the standard Android SMS database
  • If cloud sync is enabled, messages are encrypted and stored on WickedCRM servers
  • Spam reports are anonymized and aggregated

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only:

  • With your WickedCRM account (if you enable sync)
  • Phone numbers only, with spam detection services
  • When required by law

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Export: Download a copy of all your data (Settings → Export My Data)
  • Delete: Permanently delete all your data at any time (Settings → Delete All My Data)
  • Delete Synced Data: Remove data from our servers while keeping local messages
  • Disable Sync: Turn off cloud sync at any time in settings
  • Offline Use: Use the app without any data transmission

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time through the app:

  1. Open WickedCRM SMS
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear)
  3. Select "Delete All My Data" or "Delete Synced Data Only"
  4. Confirm deletion

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You can also request deletion by contacting privacy@wickedcrm.com.

Default SMS App Permissions

As a default SMS app, we require these permissions:

  • RECEIVE_SMS, SEND_SMS, READ_SMS: Core messaging functionality
  • RECEIVE_MMS: To receive picture messages
  • READ_CONTACTS: To show contact names
  • INTERNET: For spam checking and optional sync

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
